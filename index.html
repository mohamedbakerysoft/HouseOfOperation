Strategic Analysis

Understanding our position in the market

Strengths

  • Fully dedicated to developer-side operations
  • High-quality outsourcing team with real estate experience
  • Technical expertise in CRM platforms and integrations
  • Cost-saving and time-efficient for developers
  • Flexible, scalable, and reliable structure

Weaknesses

  • New company still building its market footprint
  • Requires onboarding and process mapping for each client
  • Market may need education about the outsourcing model

Opportunities

  • Surge in CRM adoption by developers
  • Increasing number of developers relying on brokers
  • Expansion across MENA (especially UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt)
  • Potential for tech integration and automation services

Threats

  • Developers hesitant to outsource core operations
  • Competition from internal operations teams or freelancers
  • Industry regulations or tech limitations
